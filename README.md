## 👋 About Me

I'm a data analyst/engineer with hands-on experience building ETL
pipelines and dimensional data models on AWS, most recently working as a
data engineer on an AWS-based streaming and batch data platform.

My work centers on turning raw, messy data into structured, queryable
warehouses and the dashboards/reports built on top of them. I care about
traceability and correctness: every pipeline I build should let you
answer "where did this number come from" with a specific query, not a
guess. I also care about optimization — making sure pipelines reliably
hit their SLAs, with particular depth in tuning AWS, Spark, and
Kafka workloads for throughput and cost.

Right now I'm rounding out my SQL/ETL background with more BI-layer work
and exploring how LLMs fit into data pipelines (structured extraction,
RAG) alongside traditional ETL.
